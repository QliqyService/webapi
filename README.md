# Qliqy | API

---
The service provides a way to enable contact with people without disclosing their personal data.
---

Use Cases
---
I left my car in a parking lot. I want to be reachable if something happens to my car — for example, if it blocks other cars from exiting or if it gets damaged.

I do not want my contact details (phone number) to be publicly available.

My pet has gone missing. I want to be notified if someone finds it, but I do not want my name, phone number, or email to be visible to everyone who sees the missing notice.

---

User Interface
---
Administrative Interface – where a user with administrator rights can monitor all activity, view statistics, and perform analytics on the collected data.

Client Interface – where a user creates QR codes (links) to contact forms, configures their parameters (visibility of personal data, required form fields, description, and contact details where notifications will be sent — email, Telegram, WhatsApp, etc.).

Contact Forms – where users without authorization fill in the required information defined during form creation and submit a message directly to the form creator.
